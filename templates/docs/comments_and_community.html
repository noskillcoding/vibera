Comments and reports

22 Aug, 2025

Vibera has built-in comments on all drops. Comments are enabled by default and let people give feedback on your projects.

How comments work

Comments appear on drops (but not pages). Users must log in to vibera to comment.

When commenting, users can show their name as:

  • Nickname - If set in account settings
  • Email address - Their vibera email
  • Anonymous - No name shown

Users can delete their own comments anytime.

Turning comments off

Use the checkbox when editing a drop, or add this to your drop content:

title: My Project
comments_enabled: false
___
Your drop content here...

Reports

Users can report drops that contain dangerous or inappropriate content. Reports help keep the platform safe.

To report a drop:

  • Users must be logged in
  • Write a brief explanation (5-100 characters)
  • Choose to show your nickname or email as reporter
  • Each user can only have one active report per drop

Reports appear at the bottom of drops in a collapsible section. Users can delete their own reports.

Other features

Vibera also has upvoting and a discovery feed for popular drops.
