Vibera Documentation

Welcome to Vibera

Vibera is a project showcase platform for developers. Share your vibe-coded projects with the world!

Getting Started

Creating Your First Drop

  1. Sign up for a Vibera account
  2. Go to Dashboard and click "New Drop"
  3. Add your project details:
    • Project title and description
    • Screenshots or demo videos
    • GitHub repository link
    • Technologies used (tools/tags)
  4. Publish and share with the community

Project Showcase Features

  • Media Support: Upload images and videos to showcase your projects
  • GitHub Integration: Link directly to your repositories
  • Tags & Tools: Categorize projects by technologies used
  • Discovery Feed: Get discovered by other developers
  • Comments & Upvotes: Engage with the community

Dashboard Customization

Personalize your Vibera experience with custom themes:

  • Default: Clean and minimal
  • MS-DOS: Retro terminal aesthetic
  • Claude: Modern and thoughtful design
  • Dark Claude: Dark and edgy theme

Custom Domains

Connect your own domain to your Vibera profile for a professional presence.

Discovery & Community

Explore trending projects, follow developers, and engage with the community through:

  • Discovery feed with trending projects
  • Tag-based project filtering
  • Upvoting and commenting system
  • Developer profiles and portfolios

Need Help?

If you have questions or need support, please reach out to us or check our community resources.
